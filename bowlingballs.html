BOWLING BALLS

Bowling Balls are the primary compenent of bowling. There are balls for straight tracetories as well as the famous curved shots. Different balls can hook in different ways depending on a persons bowling style. Balls for straight shots tend to be made of urethane or plastic with no special weight in it. I tend to prefer Storm balls just beacause of the reliability and consistent quality. If you are a beginner I'd reccomend any basic urethane ball to practice reliably getting the ball to the end of the lane. If you are moving into more intermediate play, I love this tool to help choose a ball. Note that it only reccomends Storm balls. To use the tool you will need to find information about your release such as axis tilt and axis rotation. Here are two videos by Storm to refer to when measuring these stats.
